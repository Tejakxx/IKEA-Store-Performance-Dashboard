# IKEA-Store-Performance-Dashboard
...
As part of my Data Analytics learning journey, I developed an end-to-end Business Intelligence & Power BI Dashboard project using a real-world retail sales dataset. The project involved cleaning and transforming raw data, creating interactive dashboards, and analyzing sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and financial insights to support data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Project Objectives
🔹 Analyze overall sales performance and revenue trends.
🔹 Identify top-performing products, categories, and sales representatives.
🔹 Evaluate customer purchasing behavior and segmentation.
🔹 Monitor profitability, collections, discounts, and financial performance.
🔹 Build interactive Power BI dashboards.
🔹 Generate business recommendations for data-driven decision-making.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
🔹 Microsoft Excel
🔹 Power Query
🔹 Power BI (DAX, KPIs & Interactive Dashboards)

📊 Project Workflow
Dataset Cleaning ➜ Data Transformation ➜ Power BI Dashboarding ➜ Business Insights

📈 Key Dashboards
🔹 Executive Overview
🔹 Sales Representative Performance
🔹 Product & Customer Analysis
🔹 Profitability & Business Insights
🔹 Revenue Decomposition Tree
🔹 Dashboard Summary

💡 Key Insights
🔹 Furniture emerged as the highest revenue-generating product category.
🔹 Premium customer tiers contributed significantly to overall sales and profitability.
🔹 Profit margins varied across categories, highlighting opportunities to optimize pricing strategies.
🔹 Collection rate and outstanding dues provided valuable insights into cash flow and payment performance.
🔹 Interactive dashboards enabled faster analysis across sales, products, customers, and profitability for informed business decisions.

This project strengthened my skills in Excel, Power Query, Power BI, DAX, Data Visualization, KPI Development, and Business Intelligence, while demonstrating how interactive dashboards can support sales optimization, profitability analysis, customer segmentation, and data-driven decision-making.
